Abstract. This article describes the recommended solutions for locating fire 

station zones. The shortage of fire stations is an urgent problem today, since late 

arrival to the fire place leads to economic losses and most importantly ‚Äì causes 

harm to life and health of people. According to official data of the Ministry of the 

Russian  Federation  for  Civil  Defense,  Emergencies  and  Elimination  of 

Consequences  of  Natural  Disasters,  only  for  2020  8,262  people  died  in  fires. 

Totally 439,100 fires occurred in Russia, almost 7 % fewer than in 2019. About 

70 % of deaths occur due to the effects of smoke, the rate of spread of which is 

extremely high, but there is a number of other reasons why firefighters can not 

always come to the rescue in time: not modern or faulty equipment and, of course, 

the shortage of fire depots and fire trucks. So it is necessary to find out a possible 

solution of this problem. One of the ways is simulation technology.
